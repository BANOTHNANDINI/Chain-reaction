Rules

  • The game is played on a 6x6 grid.
  • Players take turns placing their colored balls in any cell.
  • Cells have limits: 1 ball in corners, 2 balls on edges, and 3 balls in the center.
  • When a cell exceeds its limit, it explodes, distributing balls to adjacent cells.
  • Exploding balls convert the color of any opposing balls in adjacent cells.
  • The game ends when one player controls all the balls on the board.
  • Player colors are assigned randomly at the start of each game.
  • The first move of each player cannot end the game.
